The day-to-day operations of the SSH Open Marketplace are ensured by the **Editorial Team**. During
the development phase of the portal, SSHOC members act as a proto-Editorial Team but a new and
stable team will be set up before the end of April 2022.

The concept, design and development of the SSH Open Marketplace has been carried out by SSHOC WP7
members
